Sir Narendra Karmarkar

Early Life and Education

Born in 1957 in Mumbai, India, Narendra Karmarkar showed an early aptitude for mathematics. He went on to pursue his undergraduate studies at the Indian Institute of Technology (IIT), Mumbai, before obtaining his Ph.D. in mathematics from the University of California, Berkeley in 1984.

Key Contributions

In 1984, Karmarkar introduced an algorithm for linear programming, which was a major advancement over the previously known simplex algorithm. This algorithm, now known as Karmarkar's algorithm, runs in polynomial time, making it exponentially faster for certain types of problems. His work was widely recognized and was a key factor in the development of practical solutions for large-scale optimization problems in fields like economics, engineering, and computer science.

Impact on Optimization
